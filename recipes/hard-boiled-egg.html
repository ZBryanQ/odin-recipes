Hard Boiled Egg

Description

Hard boiled eggs are a easy and nutritious meal anyone can prepare. They are yum yum in my tum tum.

Ingredients

  • Raw egg
  • Water

Steps

  1. Put desired number of eggs into a pot.
  2. Fill pot with water until eggs are almost submerged.
  3. Bring water to a boil.
  4. Turn heat to low and cover pot with a lid. Let sit for 5 minutes.
  5. Turn off heat. Let sit for a while depending on desired egg toughness.
